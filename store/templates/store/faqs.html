Typically, our local deliveries take 3 to 5 days, while international orders are delivered within 6 to 8 days. However, certain products may require additional processing time, which is indicated on their respective pages below the product descriptions.

Please note that product colors may vary slightly due to photographic lighting sources or your monitor settings. We strive for accuracy, but minor variations are possible.

Orders can be canceled within 24 hours of placement, provided the item has not yet been shipped. Please contact our customer service team immediately to request a cancellation.

Yes, we offer international shipping to most countries. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on the destination. Please check our shipping policy for more details.

Order amendments are possible within 12 hours of placement, provided the order has not been processed for shipping. Contact us with your order details and the changes you wish to make.

Yes, we accept returns within 7 days of delivery, provided the product is unused, in its original packaging, and accompanied by the original receipt. Please refer to our detailed return policy for full terms and conditions.

We offer exchanges for products within 10 days of delivery if there is a size issue or a defect. The item must be in its original condition. Please contact us to initiate an exchange.

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to the courier's website. You can use this tracking number to monitor the status of your delivery.

If your merchandise does not fit, you can exchange it for a different size within our exchange policy period (10 days). Please ensure the item is in its original condition with tags attached.

In the rare event that your merchandise arrives damaged, please contact our customer service team immediately with photos of the damage. We will arrange for a replacement or refund as per our policy.
